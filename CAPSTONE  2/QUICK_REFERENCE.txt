HOW IT WORKS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Patient books appointment:
  → Step 3 checks: Does patient have active appointment?
     YES → Error message + Redirect to Step 1 (never reach confirmation)
     NO  → Continue to Step 4

  → Step 4 (Final) checks: Does patient have active appointment?
     YES → Error message + Show form again (NO APPOINTMENT CREATED)
     NO  → Create appointment ✅


ERROR MESSAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"You already have an active appointment. Please complete or cancel 
your current appointment before booking a new one."


ACTIVE vs TERMINAL STATUSES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ACTIVE (BLOCK booking):
  • Pending Time Assignment
  • Scheduled
  • Rescheduled
  • Pending Reschedule

TERMINAL (ALLOW booking):
  • Completed
  • Cancelled
