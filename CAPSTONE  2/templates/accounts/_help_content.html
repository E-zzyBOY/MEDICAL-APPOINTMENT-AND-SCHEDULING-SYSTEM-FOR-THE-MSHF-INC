Help & Support

How do I book an appointment?

Patients can book a new appointment from the "Book Appointment" option on their dashboard, choosing a doctor, date, and available time slot.

How do I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Open "My Appointments" and use the Reschedule or Cancel action next to the appointment you'd like to change.

How do I update my profile information?

Click your name in the top-right corner, then "My Profile", and use "Edit Profile" to update your details.

Who do I contact for further assistance?

Email us at support@mshfi.com and a member of our staff will get back to you.
